day3 content contact

It is used to navigate / redirects from one source to another resource

paired tag

        the attributes of anchor tag are :
           1. href(hyper reference)--it is a url/document/destination/resource
           2.target --it is used to open the link in new tab or same tab
                 new tab-->_blank
                 same tab-->_self(default)

anchor tag consist 3 states

  1. Unvisited --blue
  2. active --red
  3. visited --violet
